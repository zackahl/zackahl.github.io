ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Zachary Kahl, welcome to my portfolio.

I'm an aspiring front-end developer and designer from Toowoomba who loves developing new websites and applications. The challenge of perfecting the balance between functionality and originality is what I enjoy most about my craft.

Creating online experiences that are both intuitive and visually engaging is my main goal. I'm constantly learning new things and improving my skills so that each project succeeds the previous.

In early 2016 I graduated from USQ with a Bachelor of Information Technology. Whilst working towards my degree I was fortunate enough to undertake a two year internship with the same university, gaining invaluable knowledge in website development, design and maintenance.

When I'm not transfixed by code, perusing Stack Overflow for answers or fighting to center elements in CSS, I enjoy discovering new music, reading novels, playing video games and exercising.
