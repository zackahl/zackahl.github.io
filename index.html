ABOUT ME

HI, MY NAME IS ZACHARY KAHL, WELCOME TO MY PORTFOLIO.

Put simply, I'm front-end developer and designer residing in Toowoomba who loves developing new websites and applications. The challenge of finding the balance between functionality and originality is what I enjoy most about my craft.

Creating online experiences that are both intuitive and visually engaging is my main goal. I'm constantly learning new things and improving my skills so that each project succeeds the previous.

In 2016 I graduated from the University of Southern Queensland with a Bachelor of Information Technology majoring in Applied Computer Science. When I'm not transfixed by code or struggling to center elements in CSS, I enjoy discovering new music, reading, playing video games and exercising.
