Rules

You have 1 minute to to write as many anagrams as you can. You must make anagrams with 4 or more letters. The anagrams must only contain the letters from the current word. A four letter anagram is worth 1 point, a five letter anagram is worth 2 points and so on.

Shortcut Keys

  • Press enter to check the anagram.
  • Press spacebar to change the word.
